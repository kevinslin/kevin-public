## Examples

- campaign: `utm_campaign=20percentpromocode`
- source: `utm_source=Facebook`
- medium: `utm_medium=socialmedia`
- content: `utm_content=sidebarlink or utm_content=headerlink`

    - used to track keyword paid for in ppc
