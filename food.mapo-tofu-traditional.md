## Tips
- need to steam tofu early
- try to not make the tofu turn to nush

- mix
- 3 tbs water
- 2 tbs cornstarch

- cook with
  - 2 tbs vegetable oil
  - 2 stalks green onion
  - 2 tsp ginger
  - 2 cloves garlic

- 4 oz pork
- 2 tbs chilli bean sauce

- 1 tsp ground peppercorns
- 1/4 tsp sesame oil
